Proxy Kad (PhD)

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher working on a European Project with an aim for "Implementation of Machine Learning for Optimization of Laser Plasma Acceleration" in PHI group under the guidance of Dr. Sandrine Dobosz at CEA-IRAMIS/LIDYL, Paris-Saclay, France. I have done my Ph.D. work in the field of laser-plasma interactions under the supervision of Prof. Arvinder Singh at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar, Punjab, India. I have around seven years of experience in theoretical/ computational physics of laser plasma interactions. In this extensive experience, I have authored numerous peer-reviewed publications, presented at national - international conferences, contributed to international collaborations, and also contributed as reviewer for leading academic journals. My background also includes teaching and mentoring in research projects.
